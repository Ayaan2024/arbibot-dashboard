⚠️  IMPORTANT WARNINGS:
──────────────────────

1. REAL MONEY AT RISK
   This is live trading with real funds. Market conditions can change rapidly.
   While profit filters protect you, losses are still possible.

2. PRIVATE KEY SECURITY
   Never share your private key. Keep .env file secure.
   Consider using separate wallet for bot trading (recommended).

3. GAS FEES
   Each trade costs gas. At high network congestion, gas can exceed profits.
   Bot automatically skips trades when gas > MAX_GAS_GWEI to prevent losses.

4. FLASH LOAN RISKS
   Flash loan trades are atomic (all-or-nothing). If execution fails,
   loan is repaid and you lose nothing except potential profit.

5. START SMALL
   Test with current settings for 48 hours.
   Only scale up after confirming consistent profitability.
